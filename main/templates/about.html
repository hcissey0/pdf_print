PDF Print allows you to upload a PDF and select your printer type (simplex or duplex). Based on your selection, it will generate a printable booklet. If you select simplex, you will receive two PDFs (front pages and back pages) to print separately. If you select duplex, you will receive one PDF suitable for duplex printing.
